2025 Tesla Model S

Released on January 20, 2025 | Manufacturer: Tesla, Inc.

The 2025 Tesla Model S is an electric luxury sedan that combines cutting-edge technology with high performance.

Performance Features

  • All-electric drivetrain
  • 0-60 mph in under 2 seconds
  • Top speed of 200 mph
  • Dual motor all-wheel drive

Battery & Range

  • Over 400 miles of range on a single charge
  • Fast charging capability
  • Long-lasting battery life with an 8-year warranty

Interior & Technology

  • 17-inch touchscreen display
  • Autopilot and Full Self-Driving capabilities
  • Premium leather and suede materials
  • Customizable lighting and interior options
